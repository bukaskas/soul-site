SOUL experiences

Things we would love to share with you

01

Kite control

Learn the basic skills for kitesurfing. We will start with how to set up your kitesurfing equipment, all the safety systems and how to launch the kite. After we cover the basics we will continue to practice in the water the basics of kite control and having good understanding what we will continue learning and practicing in next lessons if you wish becoming an independent kitesurfer

02

Bodydraging

This part of the kitesurfing course you will learn how the kite can generate power and start learning the skills to prepare for the waterstart and use the board. After this part you will be efficient kite pilot, being able to go fast and have good understanding how to use the kite to get on the board.

03

Waterstart

Last step is to combine all skills you have been practicing and use them to get on the board and get the first rides. This part involves more multitasking and getting relaxed with the kite, so can balance better and ride with ease.

SOUL AND KITESURFING

The story about how it started

Kitesurfing sport is a fresh sport that started in late 90's and started growing very fast. Egypt with reputation being one of the best spots for windsports atracted the professionals to come and train here and brought it in the beginning of the sport.

Ras Sudr was known to be one of the best spots to train and learn this sport, thus there were plenty of international athletes coming to train here and this attracted all the people to come and learn here.

This is whre we found this passion and got addicted right away, this feeling about gliding above the water freely with minimum physical effort. Being able to jump high and seeing all the beautiful scenery that Ras Sudr has to offer.

We wish you join us to enjoy this beautiful location and the sport.
